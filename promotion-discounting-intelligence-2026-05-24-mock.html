Glossary
Business Metrics +
OD (Open Discount): Customer promo code discount.
ILS (Incentive Load Service): Auto-applied checkout discount.
NOR (Net Order Revenue): GR minus promo spend.
NOR%GGR: Efficiency metric. NA benchmark ~51%.
PS%GGB: Promo Spend % of Gross Bookings. NA benchmark ~19%.
VFM: Vendor-Funded Margin — merchant-funded discount, capped at 20% of unit buy price.
Purple Price: Dynamic lower price to eligible customers. metadata.purplePriceEnabled = true.
IPA: Incentive Price Adjustment. Est. $1.3M–$3.0M NA Local opportunity.
OD Attach Rate: % of transactions where OD discount applied. Target 40–50%.
Technical Terms (Incentive Service) +
Disposition states: draft → unapproved → approved → (inactive / paused / expired)
CLOSED: Requires brand, audience, or promo code qualification.
OPEN: Available to all eligible customers.
CLOSED_MARKETING_CHANNEL: Channel-gated (email/push) without promo code.
Purple Price: Controlled via metadata.purplePriceEnabled. Enables show_lower_price logic.
Stacking: OD + ILS applied simultaneously.
