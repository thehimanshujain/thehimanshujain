Score Methodology

The four headline scores at the top of this report are computed live from the data on this page. Each is shown on a 0–100 scale. Definitions and formulas below.

Health Score
Tier-weighted rollup of ORR status across all 72 services. Each service scores GREEN = 100, YELLOW = 60, RED = 0, weighted by tier (T0 ×3, T1 ×2, T2 ×1) so critical services count more.
Σ(orr×tierWeight) / Σ(100×tierWeight)
Reliability
Average of the Reliability column from the Per-Squad ORR Scorecard (§ OpEx Scorecard), across all 7 squads. Reflects SLO attainment, error budgets and incident posture.
avg(squad.reliability)
Cost Efficiency
Average of the Cost Efficiency column from the Per-Squad ORR Scorecard. Derived from actual-vs-budget cloud spend, MoM trend, and rightsizing / idle-waste signals (Cloudability in the live report).
avg(squad.costEfficiency)
OpEx Maturity
Composite mean of all 8 operational-excellence dimensions (Reliability, Scalability, Observability, Cost, Ownership, Documentation, Automation, Security) across the 7 squads — the overall ORR maturity of the portfolio.
avg(all 8 dims × 7 squads)

Source data: Service Inventory ORR statuses (§ Service Inventory) and the Per-Squad ORR Scorecard (§ OpEx Scorecard). In the live report these dimensions come from Groupon Service Portal ORR check results; this is a mock with representative values.
