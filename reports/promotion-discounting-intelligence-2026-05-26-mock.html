Glossary

Business Metrics

OD (Open Discount): Customer promo code discount. SQL: discount_amount_loc <> 0 AND ils_discount_loc <= 0

ILS (Incentive Load Service): Auto-applied checkout discount. SQL: ils_discount_loc > 0

NOR (Net Order Revenue): gr - promo_spend

NOR%GGR: Efficiency metric. NA benchmark ~51%.

PS%GGB: Promo Spend % of Gross Bookings. NA benchmark ~19%.

VFM (Vendor-Funded Margin): Merchant-funded discount. Capped at 20% of unit buy price.

GBV / GGB: Gross Bookings Value — total transaction value before refunds.

Purple Price: Dynamic lower price to eligible customers. metadata.purplePriceEnabled = true.

IPA (Incentive Price Adjustment): Revenue opportunity from green-price vs discount optimisation. Est. $3.3M–$5.0M NA Local.

OD Attach Rate: % of transactions where OD discount applied. Target 40–50%.

Technical Terms (Incentive Service)

Disposition states: draft → unapproved → approved → (inactive / paused / expired)

CLOSED: Requires brand, audience, or promo code qualification constraint.

OPEN: Available to all eligible customers — no restriction qualifier.

CLOSED_MARKETING_CHANNEL: Channel-gated (email/push) without promo code.

Qualification constraint kinds: brand, promotionCode, audience, country, startDate, endDate, perUserRedemptionCount, redemptionCount, noPushMessaging, noMessaging
